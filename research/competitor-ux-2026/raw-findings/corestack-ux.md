## FOCUS™ Compliance
- CoreStack dashboards are FOCUS™ compliant
- Follows FinOps Foundation's FOCUS (FinOps Open Cost and Usage Specification) standard
- Normalizes cost data across cloud providers

## Design Language
- **Primary color:** Blue (#0066CC-ish)
- **Background:** White
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif
- **Hero section:** Blue gradient or solid backgrounds
- **Density:** Enterprise-level, information-dense
- **Cookie consent:** Standard banner with Reject All / Accept All Cookies

## Key Differentiators
1. Multi-cloud governance (not just cost) — includes security and compliance
2. Agentic AI for automated governance assessments
3. FOCUS™ standard compliance for cost normalization
4. Partner ecosystem focus (MSPs, resellers)
5. CLOUD-AS-CODE™ approach for policy-as-code
