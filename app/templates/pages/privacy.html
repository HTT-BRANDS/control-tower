1. Overview

Azure Governance Platform ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Name and contact information
  • Organization/tenant affiliation
  • Azure AD object ID

2.2 Usage Data

  • IP address and browser information
  • Pages visited and actions taken
  • Performance and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To authenticate users and manage access
  • To analyze usage and improve the platform
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Your Privacy Rights

4.1 Global Privacy Control (GPC)

We honor the Global Privacy Control signal. If your browser sends a Sec-GPC: 1 header, we will automatically:

  • Opt you out of analytics tracking
  • Disable marketing cookies
  • Prevent sale/sharing of your personal information

4.2 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Delete their personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

4.3 EU Residents (GDPR)

EU residents have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
